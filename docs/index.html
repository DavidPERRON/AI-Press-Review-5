40 sources. 15 minutes. What matters in AI today.

AI Press Review is a daily AI news briefing — published every morning at 7AM CET, Monday through Sunday. Each episode draws from 40+ weighted global sources, structured across six editorial pillars: AI News, Research & Breakthroughs, Use Cases, Tools & Practice, Weak Signals, and Education. Sources are selected by domain authority and editorial standards. Regulatory speculation, unverified rumors, and opinion pieces are excluded. Every Saturday: a weekly recap of the five trends that defined the week. Hosted by David Perron.
